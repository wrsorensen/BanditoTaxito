# BANDITO TAXITO — FINAL BRAND THEME

Use this as the locked branding direction for the entire app.

## App name

Bandito Taxito

## Core vibe

Vintage western corrido poster + Tex-Mex outlaw tax tracker.

## Brand style

Corrido Cash / cartel-cantina / trusted outlaw / rugged 1099 tracker.

## Main visual identity

A masked bandit/jefe in a sombrero holding 1099 paperwork and cash, paired with a rugged brindle pit-style three-legged dog wearing a red bandana.

The dog must have the missing leg fully removed, with no stubby leg.

The dog is a major mascot/anchor of the brand, not a side detail.

## Dog direction

- Three-legged brindle pit-style dog
- Red bandana
- Rugged, loyal, ride-or-die energy
- No stubby leg
- No cartoon puppy look
- Tough, serious, weathered, unbreakable

## Visual style

- Vintage western poster
- Corrido/cantina badge
- Ornate circular emblem
- Distressed ink texture
- Parchment background
- Desert night scene
- Cactus, cantina, moon/stars, roses, skulls, gold trim
- Bold outlaw tax tracker energy

## Color palette

- Midnight black
- Parchment cream
- Deep blood red
- Dark teal
- Muted gold
- Desert green
- Dusty brown

## Typography

- Bold western slab serif
- Distressed poster lettering
- Ornate badge text
- High contrast, rugged, readable

## Tagline options

- Track it. Write it. Don’t die.
- 3 legs. All heart. All hustle.
- Rugged. Loyal. Unbreakable.
- Every receipt. Every mile. Every write-off.
- Protect your stack.
- Find every write-off.
- Own your paper trail.

## App personality

Funny, edgy, Tex-Mex, outlaw, but legally tax-smart.

This is not tax evasion branding. It is about tracking 1099 income, receipts, mileage, deductions, write-offs, jobs, and tax records so users do not leave money on the table.

## UI direction

- Dark rugged theme with parchment cards and western accents
- Use deep teal, red, gold, and black
- Buttons should feel like badge/stamp elements
- Use icons for receipts, mileage, jobs, write-offs, 1099, reports, and tax stash
- The dog mascot can appear in empty states, loading screens, success screens, dashboard header, and app icon

## Final rule

Do not switch to clean corporate SaaS branding.

Keep Bandito Taxito gritty, western, funny, and memorable.
