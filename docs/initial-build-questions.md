# Initial Build Questions

Use these first. Do not bury him in the long list unless needed.

1. Are you W-2, 1099, or LLC?
2. Are taxes taken out of your check?
3. Are you paid hourly, daily, salary, or per job?
4. Do you submit a timesheet, invoice, or neither?
5. Do you drive your own vehicle for work?
6. Does the company reimburse mileage?
7. Do you buy work stuff and need receipts saved?
8. Does the company reimburse those receipts?
9. What do you forget to track the most?
10. Would you actually use this daily, or only at the end of the week?

## LLC add-ons

11. Are you planning to set up a single-member LLC?
12. Will you use a separate business bank account/card?
13. Will a CPA or tax person help you with the LLC/taxes?

## Big decisions these answer

- Do we need invoicing, or just work/time proof?
- Do we need tax set-aside math, or just tax reminders?
- Do we need reimbursement tracking?
- Do we need mileage for tax deduction, reimbursement, or both?
- Is this mainly for getting paid, staying organized, or tax backup?
